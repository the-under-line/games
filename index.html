A Chance At Victory (Random-Based RPG): How to Play

Goal of the Game

Defeat as many enemies as possible!


1.

Pick a card from the (imaginary) bag! This will be your weapon and can help you in battle!

2.

Roll the die to get your starting points! The more you have, the better.

3.

Spin the (imaginary) spinner! Make the ultimate decision: Will you attack (gain 5 points), or will you GET attacked (lose 5 points)? Or do nothing at all in the face of battle? Spin Now!
